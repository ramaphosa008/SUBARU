More than a teacher

Aloo Aloo, Mr. Musyoki😂. Nilitaka tu kusema asante. More than being my class teacher and English teacher, you were like a father figure to me in school.

When I needed something, you were always ready to avail yourself - conveying my needs to my parents, and sometimes even catering for them in their absence. When I was low, you noticed and called me aside to ask if I was doing alright. You gave me relentless advice and inspiration, and you never gave up on me even when I gave you every reason to 😂 Yes, ulikuwa nyoka sometimes, but you were also the closest thing I had to a friend.

Saying thank you feels like an understatement, but please know your care and efforts will forever be remembered. I hope blessings follow you for the rest of your days.

Asante sana, Mwalimu.
