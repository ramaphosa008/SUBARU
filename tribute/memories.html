Through the years

Moments that shall forever be remembered and cherished

A story told in moments

Four years hold more memories than any page can carry, but these are the ones we will tell our own children about one day.

Form One
The first day he knew our names

Most teachers don't even know the names of their students, but you made it a point to learn them all. You would greet us by name every morning, and that small gesture made us feel seen and valued. It was the start of a relationship that would shape our high school experience. We weren't a promising class at first but you never gave up on us, and you always believed in our potential even when we didn't. That belief was the foundation of everything that came after.

Form Two
The exam week pep talk

Every time we were about to sit for exams, you would gather us in the classroom and tell us: "We are capable of greatness if only we believe in ourselves and put our minds to it." And then you would smile, and we would feel ready.

Form Three
Sports day and the cheer that surprised us

You were the loudest voice in the crowd. Nobody expected that. But there you were, cheering for every student regardless of house, because that was just who you were. Your class followed your example and became one of the most spirited on the field, it bred the best of the best in sports and that will never be forgotten.

Form Four
The last class

You conviced our parents to fund a last class party for us. You used that opportunity to tell us that we were ready for the world, and that we should never forget the lessons we had learned in your class. You reminded us that we were capable of achieving anything we set our minds to, and that we should always strive for excellence in everything we do.
